## Reviewer notes draft

Thank you for reviewing bracketball.

bracketball is a mobile-first March Madness draft and pool app. Users create drafts, join pools, follow live standings, view brackets, and receive optional mobile notifications for pool updates.

Review account:
- Email: `[reviewer-account-email]`
- Password: `[reviewer-account-password]`

Suggested review path:
1. Sign in with the review account.
2. Open `Pools` and tap the seeded review pool to open its leaderboard.
3. From the leaderboard, review standings and the `Share Invite` action.
4. Use the bottom navigation to open the pool bracket.
5. Open `Drafts` to view a saved draft.
6. Open `Profile` to confirm account editing, avatar upload behavior, and mobile notification settings.

Notes:
- The app uses a hosted service for live app content and account data inside the native mobile shell.
- Push notifications are optional and can be enabled from `Profile`.
- Universal links to pool routes open the native app when installed.
- Support URL: `https://www.bracketball.io/support`
- Privacy Policy: `https://www.bracketball.io/privacy`
- Terms: `https://www.bracketball.io/terms`
